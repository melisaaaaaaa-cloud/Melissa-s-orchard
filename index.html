About Us

Melissa's orchard is a family-owned business that has been operating for over 20 years. We are passionate about growing high-quality fruits and vegetables using sustainable farming practices. Our orchard is located in a picturesque countryside setting, where we cultivate a wide range of produce, including apples, strawberries, lettuce, and more. We are committed to providing our customers with fresh and delicious products while also caring for the environment.
